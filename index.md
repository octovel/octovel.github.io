# Octovel

A small group of volunteer developers building and maintaining free, fast and reliable open-source tools.

Our mission is to improve the developer experience by building lightweight and efficient SDKs and collaboration tools available across different systems. We follow a straightforward philosophy, quality over quantity. Our products are 100% free, and collected funds are redistributed to our best contributors and partnered organizations.
